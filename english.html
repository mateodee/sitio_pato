I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina. From a very young age I have been passionate about art. I’ve always painted and sang, I am also an engineer, but a few years ago I decided to focus exclusively on painting. Although I have worked on different themes using different techniques, a few years ago I decided to dedicate myself to paint my own interpretation of short stories such us Little Red Riding Hood, Beauty and the Beast, Puss in Boots and Alicia (among others). My main goal is to reach, through these paintings, the most childish part that we all have inside. But at the same time, I also try to mobilize the viewer with the psychological side of my works. I work using different techniques: oil, collage, mixed media, pastels and stained glass lacquer.
